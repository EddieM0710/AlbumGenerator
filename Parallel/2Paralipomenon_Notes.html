THE SECOND BOOK OF PARALIPOMENON

chap. 13 ver. 2 Michaia. . .Alias Maacha. Her father had also two names, viz., Absalom, or Abessalom, and Uriel.

chap. 13 ver. 5 A covenant of salt. . .That is, a firm and perpetual covenant. See Num. 18.19.

chap. 16 ver. 1 Six and thirtieth year of his kingdom. . .That is, of the kingdom of Juda, taking the date of it from the beginning of the reign of Reboam.

chap. 18 ver. 19 Who shall deceive, etc. . .See the annotations, 3 Kings 22.

chap. 21 ver. 17 Joachaz. . .Alias Ochozias.

chap. 22 ver. 2 Forty-two, etc. . .Divers Greek Bibles read thirty-two, agreeably to 4 Kings 8.17.

chap. 23 ver. 5 To the sabbath. . .That is, to perform in your weeks the functions of your office, or the weekly watches.

chap. 28 ver. 19 For he had stripped it of help. . .That is, Achaz stripped the kingdom of Juda of the divine assistance by his wickedness, and by his introducing idolatry.

chap. 30 ver. 3 The host of heaven. . .The sun, moon, and stars.

chap. 33 ver. 3 The host of heaven. . .The sun, moon, and stars.

chap. 36 ver. 9 Eight years old. . .He was associated by his father to the kingdom, when he was but eight years old; but after his father's death, when he reigned alone, he was eighteen years old. 4 Kings 24.8.

THE THIRD EPISTLE OF ST. JOHN THE APOSTLE

chap. 1 ver. 4 No greater grace. . .that is nothing that gives me greater joy and satisfaction.

chap. 1 ver. 7 Taking nothing of the Gentiles. . .These ministers of the gospel are commended by St. John, who took nothing from the Gentiles, lest they should seem to preach in order to get money by it.

chap. 1 ver. 10 Diotrephes who loveth. . .This man seemeth to be in power, but not a friend to the faithful; therefore this part of the letter might be an admonition to him from the apostle.

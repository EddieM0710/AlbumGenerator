THE PROPHECY OF DANIEL

chap. 1 ver. 2 His god. . .Bel or Belus, the principal idol of the Chaldeans.

chap. 1 ver. 8 Be defiled, etc. . .Viz., either by eating meat forbidden by the law, or which had before been offered to idols.

chap. 1 ver. 12 Pulse. . .That is, pease, beans, and such like.

chap. 2 ver. 1 The second year. . .Viz., from the death of his father Nabopolassar; for he had reigned before as partner with his father in the empire.

chap. 2 ver. 2 The Chaldeeans. . .That is, the astrologers, that pretended to divine by stars.

chap. 2 ver. 39 Another kingdom. . .Viz., that of the Medes and Persians. Ibid. Third kingdom. . .Viz., that of Alexander the Great.

chap. 2 ver. 40 The fourth kingdom, etc. . .Some understand this of the successors of Alexander, the kings of Syria and Egypt, others of the Roman empire, and its civil wars.

chap. 2 ver. 44 A kingdom. . .Viz., the kingdom of Christ in the Catholic Church which cannot be destroyed.
